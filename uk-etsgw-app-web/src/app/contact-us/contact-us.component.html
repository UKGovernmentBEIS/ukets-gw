Contact the UK Emissions Trading Scheme reporting helpdesk

If you need help accessing or using the UK Emissions Trading Scheme reporting service, please contact your regulator.

Aviation Operators (UK ETS / CORSIA)

  • Operators with a registered office address or place of residence in England: etaviationhelp@environment-agency.gov.uk
  • Operators (UK ETS only) with a registered office address or place of residence outside the UK: etaviationhelp@environment-agency.gov.uk
  • Operators with a registered office address in Scotland: emission.trading@sepa.org.uk
  • Operators with a registered office address in Northern Ireland: emissions.trading@daera-ni.gov.uk
  • Operators with a registered office address in Wales: GHGHelp@naturalresourceswales.gov.uk

Installation Operators (including offshore)

  • Operators with a geographical location in England: ethelp@environment-agency.gov.uk
  • Operators with a geographical location in Scotland: emission.trading@sepa.org.uk
  • Operators with a geographical location in Northern Ireland: emissions.trading@daera-ni.gov.uk
  • Operators with a geographical location in Wales: GHGHelp@naturalresourceswales.gov.uk
  • Operators of offshore installations with a geographical location on the UK Continental Shelf, including relevant (non-devolved) controlled waters and adjacent territorial sea areas: opred@energysecurity.gov.uk

Maritime Operators

  • Operators based internationally or those with a registered address in England: etmaritimehelp@environment-agency.gov.uk
  • Operators with a registered office address in Scotland: emission.trading@sepa.org.uk
  • Operators with a registered office address in Northern Ireland: emissions.trading@daera-ni.gov.uk
  • Operators with a registered office address in Wales: GHGHelp@naturalresourceswales.gov.uk
