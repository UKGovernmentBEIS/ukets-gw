Manage your UK Emissions Trading Scheme reporting

This service is for operators, regulators and verifiers who carry out activities covered by the UK Emissions Trading Scheme (UK ETS) or the Carbon Offsetting and Reduction Scheme for International Aviation (CORSIA). Installations in Northern Ireland will also use this service for the European Union Emissions Trading System (EU ETS).

This service is also applicable for the Waste Activities Voluntary Scheme for Greenhouse Gas Emissions, although some references and data requests may not be relevant to waste operators.

Installation, aircraft, aeroplane and maritime operators should use this service to:

  • apply for an emissions monitoring plan or permit
  • manage emissions monitoring plans and permits
  • submit annual reports

Regulators should use this service to assess applications and reports.
Verifiers should use this service to verify reports.
